ETO issues on-chain index tokens that provide self-custodied, permissionless exposure to real-world markets, with stablecoin entry/exit and on-chain settlement.

MAANG is ETO's flagship index token: one ERC-20 that tracks an unweighted average of Meta, Amazon, Apple, Nvidia, and Google (Alphabet). It provides price exposure onlyit does not represent share ownership, does not pay dividends, and provides no shareholder rights.

ETO is wallet-based (permissionless access). You interact using any EVM-compatible wallet rather than a traditional account flow. You are responsible for complying with your local laws and regulations.

MAANG runs on an Avalanche-powered L1 subnet designed specifically for ETO's index products. ETO's swap experience supports USDC from multiple networks with automatic routing, so you don't have to manage manual bridging steps.

Staking terms can vary by program (including potential lockups, cooldowns, and reward schedules). The current terms are displayed in-app before you commit your assets.

ETO uses layered defenses including continuous auditing, oracle validation, rate limits and circuit breakers, administrative timelocks, and ongoing monitoring and incident response processes.

Key risks include market risk (prices can move against you), technical risk (smart contract, oracle, or infrastructure failure), counterparty risk (stablecoin risk with USDC), and external factors (administrative, governance, or regulatory risks). ETO does not provide investment advice.

There are two common paths: Hold (if the index token's reference price rises, the token's market price can rise too) or Stake (stake supported assets to earn variable rewardsrates and terms are shown in the app). Nothing is guaranteedprincipal is at risk.
