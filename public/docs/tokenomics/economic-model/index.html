Expansion Auction Mechanism

When milestones are reached, new tokens are distributed through sealed-bid auctions:

PhaseDurationDescription
Auction Announcement7 daysNotice period with full auction details
Bidding Period24 hoursSealed bid submission window
Price Discovery—Dutch auction with oracle-anchored floor price
Allocation—Pro-rata distribution based on bid prices
Settlement—Automatic token delivery and USDC collection

The auction mechanism prevents discount dilution:

PSM Fee Structure

The PSM applies variable fees based on utilization and market conditions:

Base Fee Calculation

fbase=f0×(1+κ⋅Ut)f_{\text{base}} = f_0 \times \left(1 + \kappa \cdot U_t\right)fbase​=f0​×(1+κ⋅Ut​)

Where f0f_0f0​ is the base fee rate (e.g., 0.50%) and κ\kappaκ is the utilization multiplier.

Volatility Adjustment

During high volatility periods, fees increase:

fvol=fbase×(1+γ⋅σrealized)f_{\text{vol}} = f_{\text{base}} \times \left(1 + \gamma \cdot \sigma_{\text{realized}}\right)fvol​=fbase​×(1+γ⋅σrealized​)

Final Fee Calculation

ftotal=min⁡(fvol,fmax⁡)×Snotionalf_{\text{total}} = \min(f_{\text{vol}}, f_{\max}) \times S_{\text{notional}}ftotal​=min(fvol​,fmax​)×Snotional​

Reserve-Adjusted PSM Threshold

The PSM threshold adapts based on reserve levels:

θadaptive=θbase×(1−0.5⋅Ureserve)\theta_{\text{adaptive}} = \theta_{\text{base}} \times \left(1 - 0.5 \cdot U_{\text{reserve}}\right)θadaptive​=θbase​×(1−0.5⋅Ureserve​)

This makes PSM more conservative as reserves are depleted, protecting the system from over-extension.
